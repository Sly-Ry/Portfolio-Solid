About Me

My name is Ryan Alexander Williams and I am a Full Stack Web Developer with a background in Theater and Customer Service. I've worked in customer service for most of my life and have directed theatre productions in Austin. I taught myself a bit of Python before deciding to enlist in the UT Coding Bootcamp. I spent six months developing skills ranging from HTML to reactjs while simultaneously working a full time job on the side. After those six months, I received my certification in Full Stack Web Development. I utilize creativity and problem-solving to build user-friendly applications incorporating the MERN Stack. I'm known around the workplace for my unparalleled work ethic, impeccable attention to detail, and being a versatile team player capable of undertaking any project with a single mindset, “Never let an experience stop you from growing.”
